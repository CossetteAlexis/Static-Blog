POSITION PAPER : ANGKAS SHOULD BE LEGALIZED

In the Philippines, motorcycles are one of the most convenient form of transportation. The fact that it doesn't always stop to fetch passengers unlike PUV's who stops to fetch passengers even if it is full.

According to the PHILPAD, majority of full-time Angkas riders now earn as much as P1,500 per day while those who do it part-time earn an average of P500 per day. There is really an extra income opportunity for Angkas drivers.

Why Angkas should be legalized?

Angkas is an easy way of transportation since it can easily pass through the narrow roads and everywhere you go there are always a motorcycles who waits for passengers.

Angkas is the source of income to many people. If Angkas will be illegalized many will lose there job and many family will starve because Angkas is the only source of their income.

There are so many people who don't have jobs then it will increase if they will illegalized Angkas. How can they solve it if they can't give jobs to the unemployed Filipinos?

Student like me always ride habal-habal every school days and I can really say that if it will be illegalize many will suffer not just only the drivers and their family but also to those people who is dependent to the motorcycles.

Every people who rides in habal-habal or in Angkas knows very well the risks on riding motorcycles but it is the most convenient form of transportation to get to work, school, and to the other destination. It is right to legalize Angkas as a form of transportation and make it a public vehicle. There is no such place where accidents don't happen. Just wear your helmet wherever you ride Angkas for your safety.

Posted By: Allana Jane Dungog
